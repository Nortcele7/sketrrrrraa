Sketraa revolutionizes home design by transforming your ideas into reality. Powered by user prompts, Sketraa crafts personalized house plans and stunning 3D models, combining innovation and creativity to bring your dream home to life effortlessly.
SKETRAA
Sketraa is a visionary platform tailored to Nepal, where tradition meets innovation in home design. Specializing in crafting house plans inspired by the rich heritage of Nepali mud and brick architecture, Sketraa preserves the timeless charm of traditional homes while incorporating modern engineering techniques to ensure safety and durability. Understanding Nepal’s unique geography and seismic challenges, Sketraa focuses on creating earthquake-resistant house plans that prioritize both structural integrity and aesthetic appeal. Whether it’s a quaint home in the hills, a traditional Newari-style courtyard house, or a modern twist on vernacular designs, Sketraa blends cultural authenticity with functionality. Perfect for Nepali homeowners, architects, and communities, Sketraa emphasizes sustainable and locally sourced materials, ensuring your home harmonizes with the natural environment and stands as a testament to Nepal’s rich architectural legacy. Let Sketraa bring your dream home to life, rooted in tradition and built for the future.
